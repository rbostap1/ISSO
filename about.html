About ISSO

Learn about our organization, mission, and impact

Who We Are

The Information Systems Student Organization (ISSO) is a student-led organization dedicated to bridging the gap between academic learning and professional practice in the field of information systems.

Founded with the vision of creating a supportive community for students passionate about technology, ISSO has grown to become a cornerstone of professional development on campus. We provide a platform for students to explore their interests, develop essential skills, and connect with industry professionals.

Our diverse membership includes students from various academic backgrounds, all united by a common interest in information systems and technology. Whether you're a business major interested in IT management or a computer science student exploring enterprise systems, ISSO welcomes you.

"ISSO transformed my college experience. The connections I made and skills I developed here have been invaluable for my career."

- ISSO Alumni

Our Foundation

Our Mission

To foster a community of students passionate about information systems, providing them with the skills, knowledge, and connections needed to succeed in the ever-evolving technology landscape.

Our Vision

To be the premier student organization for aspiring IT professionals, recognized for excellence in education, community engagement, and producing industry-ready graduates.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Collaboration, Innovation, and Community Service guide everything we do as an organization committed to student success.

Our History

Founded
The Beginning

ISSO was established by a group of passionate students who recognized the need for a dedicated community focused on information systems. What started as a small study group has grown into a thriving organization.

Growth
Expansion & Recognition

Over the years, ISSO expanded its programs and partnerships, earning recognition from the university and industry partners. Our events began attracting top recruiters and speakers from leading technology companies.

Today
A Thriving Community

Today, ISSO stands as one of the most active student organizations on campus, with over 200 active members, 50+ annual events, and partnerships with 30+ industry leaders.

What We Believe
